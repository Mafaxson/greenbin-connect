Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 24, 2026

GreenBin Connect, a project developed by AfrikSpark Tech Solutions, respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information.

1. Who We Are

GreenBin Connect is a technology-driven environmental platform that helps individuals, communities, businesses, and public institutions report waste issues, improve waste collection, support recycling, and promote cleaner cities.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Location data (when submitted by users)
  • Waste reports, messages, images, or videos
  • Device/browser information
  • Usage analytics
  • Communication preferences

3. How We Use Information

We use your information to:

  • Process waste reports and service requests
  • Improve waste collection and environmental response
  • Communicate updates and notifications
  • Improve our website, apps, and services
  • Generate analytics for service planning
  • Prevent fraud, abuse, or misuse
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Data Sharing

We may share limited information with:

  • Waste collection partners
  • Recycling partners
  • Government or municipal partners
  • Technology service providers
  • Legal authorities when required by law

We do not sell personal information.

5. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and administrative safeguards to protect user data from unauthorized access, misuse, or loss.

6. Cookies and Analytics

Our website may use cookies and analytics tools to improve performance, understand visitor behavior, and enhance user experience.

7. User Rights

Users may request to:

  • Access their data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete personal data
  • Withdraw consent where applicable
  • Contact us regarding privacy concerns

8. Children's Privacy

We aim to protect children's privacy. If information relating to children is submitted unintentionally, we will take reasonable steps to manage it responsibly.

9. Third-Party Links

Our website or platform may contain third-party links. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of other websites.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updated versions will be posted on this page.
