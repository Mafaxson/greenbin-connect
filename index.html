The Reality on the Ground — Freetown

The Problem

Freetown's waste crisis is urgent

Unmanaged waste in Freetown's communities blocks drains, causes flooding, and breeds disease — while residents have no way to report hazards or schedule collection.

🦟

Disease outbreaks

Rotting waste creates breeding grounds for mosquitoes and bacteria — directly causing cholera and malaria, especially in children.

🌊

Flooding every rainy season

Waste-blocked drainage channels cause severe flooding across Freetown neighborhoods, destroying homes and businesses.

📡

No system, no response

Communities have no channel to report waste hazards. Authorities lack the data to respond. GreenBin Connect closes this gap.
